## Long Description (Max 16,000 characters)
YT DeepNote is the ultimate note-taking companion for students, researchers, and lifelong learners on YouTube. Stop switching between tabs and losing context. With YT DeepNote, a powerful rich-text Markdown editor floats seamlessly over your YouTube video, allowing you to capture insights the moment they happen.

**Key Features:**
- 📝 **In-Video Floating Editor:** Take notes without ever leaving the video player. Move, resize, and dock the editor anywhere on your screen.
- ⏱️ **Timestamped Bookmarks:** One click drops a clickable timestamp bookmark. Click a bookmark to instantly jump the video to that exact moment.
- 📸 **Quick Screenshots:** Capture screenshots of the video directly into your notes with a single click. Images are resizable right inside the editor!
- 🔄 **Notion Sync:** Automatically push your perfectly formatted Markdown notes, complete with images and timestamps, directly to your Notion workspace.
- 🎨 **Beautiful UI:** A sleek, glassmorphic dark-mode design that looks natively integrated with YouTube.
- 📁 **Markdown Export:** Don't use Notion? Export your notes and screenshots instantly to a clean, highly readable Markdown (.md) file for Obsidian, Logseq, or VSCode.
- 📚 **Playlist Progress Tracking:** Seamlessly tracks your progress through YouTube playlists, automatically checking off videos as you finish them.

**Privacy First:**
Your notes belong to you. All draft data is stored locally in your browser. YT DeepNote only connects to external services (like Notion) when you explicitly provide an API token.
