# Privacy Policy for YT DeepNote

**Last Updated:** May 28, 2026

YT DeepNote ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the YT DeepNote browser extension.

## 1. Data We Collect and How We Use It
YT DeepNote is designed to be a local-first extension. 

- **Notes, Screenshots & Bookmarks:** All of your notes, captured screenshots, and video bookmarks are stored completely locally on your device within your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.
- **Notion Integration:** If you choose to enable the Notion Sync feature, you must manually provide your own Notion API Token. This token is stored locally on your device. When you trigger a sync, your browser communicates directly with Notion's official API (`api.notion.com`) to securely transmit your notes. We do not act as a middleman and never see your token or notes.

## 2. Third-Party Services
Our extension interacts with the following third-party services based on your usage:
- **YouTube:** To overlay the note-taking interface and capture video timestamps/screenshots.
- **Notion:** (Optional) To sync your notes directly to your Notion workspace using your personal API key.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion
Because your data is stored locally on your machine, you have complete control over it. You can delete your notes or uninstall the extension at any time, which will permanently wipe the data from your browser's storage.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
